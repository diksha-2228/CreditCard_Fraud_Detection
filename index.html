Performance Insights

The **Random Forest (Tuned)** model is the clear winner for production use. In evaluation:

  • It captures 83 out of 98 actual fraud cases.
  • It triggers only 41 false alarms, yielding a robust 0.7477 F1 score.
  • The baseline **Logistic Regression** catches 90 frauds but creates 1,439 false alarms, making it unworkable in practice due to the massive cost of investigation.
Why accuracy is not reported: Predicting "Legitimate" for all transactions yields 99.83% accuracy due to severe class imbalance (0.17% fraud rate). High accuracy is a false indicator of success here; we optimize strictly for F1 Score and Precision-Recall.
